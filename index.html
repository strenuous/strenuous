Our Services

Housekeeping Services

Our House Keeping services are available 24x7 and seven days a week under the complete supervision of accredited Quality Management and Time and Attendance auditing systems. To ensure higher levels of service, our supervisors perform frequent visits to the site as well as audits. Thus, we offer complete support to our client and want to make them comfortable and satisfied with our world class services.

Total Security Solutions

All businesses require a security solution that is fit for purpose, robust and, importantly in the current economic climate, cost effective. Advances in security technology now make it more likely than ever before that the correct security strategy will be to utilise both physical security and electronic security systems in a fully integrated security approach.

Monitoring Services

Remote CCTV is an effective and economical solution to securing premises or high value items around-the-clock; consolidating video, access control and audio and alarm systems to create a single point of security management. Our centrally based UK monitoring station delivers next generation monitoring services with state-of-the-art CCTV transmission and recording technology to manage and respond to CCTV alarms generated from protected sites, 24/7, 365 days a year.

Maintenance Services

Without regular maintenance even the most effective security equipment will deteriorate, so it's important that a qualified technician checks your electronic security system at least annually. This will ensure your equipment is working properly, meets current standards, and is less likely to fail or develop faults in the future. Our maintenance package also guarantees that if you do have a problem, a fully qualified technician can be on site within four hours if required.

Access Control

Whether you are embarking on a new access control system or considering migrating and/or enhancing your existing access control system, we offer impartial advice using only best of breed products that are designed to meet the ever changing security standards. Strenuous's access control systems range from standalone systems through to complex multi-site installations including audio/video entry systems, automatic gates, electronic barriers, and turnstiles.
